Reporting line — current and recommended

Reporting line recommendation

Current: Jack reports to Shikha Sohal (Operations Manager) during onboarding. This is appropriate while Jack is getting established in the role and building familiarity with RSP systems and customers.

Recommended future state: Jack should report to Sridhar Bhattacharjee (Service Manager) once onboarding is complete — typically 3–6 months. The Service Controller role is defined by service KPIs (response time, resolution time, backlog ageing, billable capture) that sit entirely within Sridhar's domain. Shikha's ops metrics (order velocity, payroll) are secondary obligations, not the primary accountability structure.

Rationale from the position agreement: The PD places the Service Controller within Sridhar's service Scorecard — response time, FTF enablement, and billable capture are all service management outcomes. Shikha retains accountability for operations lead measures (open orders, payroll) from any Vanuatu entity role, but Jack's primary line manager should be the person who can coach the service dimension of the role.

Transition signal: Jack is ready to move to Sridhar's reporting line when the Processing Invoice backlog is consistently below 5 cases, the WOI stale case count is consistently zero, and Jack is demonstrating proactive case logging discipline without prompting.
