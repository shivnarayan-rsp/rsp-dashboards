Cost Per Copy SLA — What It Means for You

Your solution includes a Cost Per Copy Service Level Agreement covering both black and colour prints. This is more than a service contract — it's a fundamentally simpler way to manage your print environment.

No Surprise Toner Bills
Toner, parts, and labour are all covered. You pay per page — nothing more. No call-outs, no consumable invoices.
Priority On-Site Response
SLA customers receive priority service. When your device needs attention, our technician comes to you.
Fully Predictable Costs
A fixed cost per page makes budget forecasting straightforward. Finance teams can plan accurately — no variables.

How billing works

Usage-based, billed annually

Meters are read at the end of each 12-month billing period. You're invoiced based on actual pages printed — black and colour tracked separately. A4 prints at up to 10% average coverage count as one page. A3 prints are charged at twice the A4 rate. That's it — no other charges.
