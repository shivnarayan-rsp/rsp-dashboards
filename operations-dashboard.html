Order fulfilment velocity
Orders move from receipt to invoice without delay or rework
CSF 2

CSF 3
Payroll cycle reliability
Island entity payroll is processed accurately and on time every cycle
CSF 4
Data & systems accuracy
BMS, Unleashed, and Xero records are clean and current (supports all WIGs)

EOS + 4DX at RSP

RSP uses 4DX (4 Disciplines of Execution) for operational focus — WIGs, lead measures, and the weekly Scorecard. EOS provides the meeting cadence (L10) and accountability structure (Rocks, Issues List, IDS). The two frameworks complement each other: 4DX tells you what to measure and score weekly; EOS tells you how to run the conversations around those numbers.

WIGs replace "Rocks" from standard EOS. The Scorecard tab is your weekly L10 Scorecard. Issues that appear on this dashboard and can't be resolved by Shikha alone go on the L10 Issues List for IDS.

Weekly is the primary accountability layer — Scorecard, WIG progress, and L10 prep. Monthly is strategic — WIG trajectory, what's working, what needs to go on next quarter's WIG list.

03

Running the L10 Scorecard

The L10 Scorecard is a 5-minute agenda item — not a reporting session. Each lead measure gets a green or red. Red tiles get one sentence: cause and action. The goal is clarity, not justification. The Scorecard is not a performance review — it is a weekly calibration of whether the team is on trajectory for the WIGs.

Green tile

At or above target. Say it aloud, acknowledge the team member responsible, move on. No discussion needed. The L10 is not the place to explain green tiles.

Red tile

Off target. One sentence: what caused it, what action you're taking this week. If red for 2 consecutive weeks with no movement — add to Issues List for IDS. Do not accept red tiles as normal.

The 4DX principle on lead measures

Lag measures tell you if you won. Lead measures tell you if you're going to win. The Scorecard tracks lead measures — the things Shikha controls this week that predict whether the WIGs will be hit by June. If a lead measure is consistently green but the WIG progress bar isn't moving, the lead measure is wrong. Raise it at the quarterly WIG review.
