Baseline note

The baseline above is derived from Xero pay batch data (created date vs occurred date). Negative lead times in the raw data indicate SyncHub ingested historic records after they were posted — the "created to updated" delta (1–5 days) is the more reliable proxy for processing lag. Once GoPayroll MCP is live, timestamps will be captured at submission rather than back-synced, giving clean real-time measurement. The 3.7-day average should be treated as the floor for the WIG 03 baseline, not the ceiling — actual processing time may be longer if pre-submission steps (timesheet collection, approvals) are not yet captured.
