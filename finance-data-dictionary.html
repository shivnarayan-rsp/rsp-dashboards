FX Rates Applied
All Pacific entity values are stored in Synchub in local currency. The dashboard multiplies each local value by the rate below to produce an NZD equivalent. Rates are static end-of-month mid-market rates — they are not live and must be updated manually each month. A 5% movement in VUV/NZD, for example, changes Vanuatu's AR value by ~5% in NZD terms without any underlying change in invoiced amounts.

AR Ageing — Source & Bucket Definitions
Xero table: Invoice · Filter: Type = 'ACCREC' AND Status IN ('AUTHORISED', 'PARTIALLYPAID') AND IsDeleted = FALSE
Value field: AmountDue — this is the outstanding balance after any partial payments or credits. It is NOT the original invoice total.
Age calculation: Based on DueDate compared to today's date in NZ Standard Time. Age = today − DueDate (in days). A positive value means overdue.

Note: The dashboard does not include a 0–30 day overdue bucket. Invoices 1–30 days overdue appear in the drilldown but are grouped into the 30–60 bucket range for summary purposes. This is consistent with the prior manual dashboard convention.

DSO — Days Sales Outstanding
Formula: DSO = (Total AR ÷ 90-Day Revenue) × 90

90-Day Revenue definition: Sum of SubTotal (excl. tax) from Invoice table where Type = 'ACCREC', Status IN ('AUTHORISED', 'PAID'), and invoice Date falls within the trailing 90 days from today. This is invoiced revenue, not cash received.

Interpretation: DSO represents the average number of days it takes to collect payment after an invoice is raised. A DSO of 60 means on average customers take 60 days to pay.
⚠ RSP DSO caveat: RSP's Xero account contains intercompany invoices raised against Pacific Island subsidiaries. These inflate both Total AR and 90-Day Revenue, which can distort the DSO calculation. The Finance Manager should confirm whether intercompany invoices should be excluded before using RSP DSO for external benchmarking.

Known Limitations & Items for Finance Review
ItemDescriptionImpactAction Required
Alpine Print not in Synchub Alpine's Xero account is not connected to Synchub. All Alpine figures are manually sourced from the weekly finance dashboard spreadsheet. Alpine figures do not refresh automatically. If the spreadsheet is not updated, Alpine data may be stale. Connect Alpine Xero to Synchub, or update the DATA object in finance-dashboard.html each week.
RSP intercompany invoices RSP raises intercompany invoices against Pacific subsidiaries in Xero. These are included in both AR and 90-day revenue figures. RSP's Total AR, DSO, and 90+ day overdue figures are inflated relative to third-party customer AR. Finance Manager to confirm whether intercompany should be excluded. If so, a contact filter can be added to the Synchub queries.
FX rates are static Rates are updated manually at month-end and hardcoded in the dashboard. Mid-month currency movements are not reflected. NZD values may diverge from actuals if there is significant FX volatility. Update FX rates in finance-dashboard.html at start of each month. Future enhancement: pull live rates from an FX API.
DSO trend is approximate Prior DSO uses the current AR snapshot against prior-period revenue (91–180 days ago), not a true point-in-time prior AR balance. The trend arrow shows direction reliably but the magnitude (e.g. +12 days) may overstate or understate the actual change. For precise trend, maintain a weekly AR snapshot table. This is planned as part of the BC/data lake migration.
Synchub sync lag Synchub mirrors Xero on a scheduled basis. During a sync job, queries may time out. Data may be up to a few hours behind Xero. Dashboard figures may not reflect same-day transactions in Xero. Refresh the dashboard after the nightly Synchub sync completes (typically overnight NZ time).
AmountDue vs invoice total The dashboard uses AmountDue (outstanding balance) not the original invoice SubTotal for AR ageing. A partially paid invoice shows only the remaining balance, which is correct for working capital purposes but differs from the original invoice value. No action required — this is the correct treatment.
