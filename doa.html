Sequential Approval Rule

All approvals must be endorsed at each required authority level before progressing to the next level.

Example — Bad-debt write-off of $15,000: First approved by the Finance Manager → then David Apfel (Fractional CFO) → finally CEO. Each level must sign off before the next is engaged.

Footnotes & Definitions

* Check the Notes / Exceptions column for any override or exception. All exceptions must be logged in the decision tracker with reason and approver.
** Non-standard terms refers to: extended payment, unusual SLAs, penalties, bespoke items, or long-term contracts exceeding 60 months.
BAB Business Advisory Board. Required as co-approver on all CAPEX above $25,000.
CFO Fractional CFO role held by David Apfel. Contact via Finance Manager (Minoo Sigan) for scheduling.
*** Loan machine approval by Senior Engineer requires: (a) recorded in Salesforce/service system with reason and expected return date; (b) customer is not on stop-credit (or has an approved exception); and (c) the loan unit is available and does not jeopardise another committed customer.
**** Parts fitting approval by Senior Engineer requires: (a) within standard service plan or contracted entitlement; or parts cost ≤ $500 and labour is within normal service parameters; and (b) it is the best-value option versus replacement or escalation.
