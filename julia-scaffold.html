Five structural obligations. These are not quarterly goals — they are the ongoing requirements for the Tonga branch to function. A failure in any one of these creates rework for NZ, lost revenue, or compliance exposure.

CSF 01
Invoicing governance
Every billable job in Tonga is invoiced within 2 working days of completion or proof-of-delivery. Correct pricing, correct tax, correct customer reference. Right-first-time — no credits issued due to internal error.

Growth path

Julia's current role is the Accounts/Administration Officer for Tonga. The Operations Lead (Branch Manager) role is a natural progression — she is already performing several of the required functions. This section maps the gap.

Operations Lead — Tonga Future state
The Operations Lead role is a multiplier — measured on the team's outputs, system quality, and resilience, not personal firefighting. Julia currently owns the admin and billing-to-cash functions. The step up requires taking ownership of service delivery, inventory availability, compliance, and people capability as a system — not as a series of individual tasks.
What Julia already does
End-to-end invoicing governance, AR/credit control, stop-credit gating, meter reading collection, payroll coordination, document repository quality.
What the Operations Lead adds
Branch operating rhythm ownership (daily KPIs, team meetings, escalation handling), service and inventory coordination as a system, SOP library ownership, compliance and risk management, staff capability uplift, and continuous improvement cadence (1 measurable improvement per quarter).
KPI uplift required
Service SLA performance (response + resolution targets met monthly), inventory accuracy ≥98% and zero critical stockouts, quality and rework ≤1%, 100% staff with documented SOPs and quarterly skills plan.
Suggested development steps
Participate in Sridhar's service Scorecard reviews (Tonga). Co-develop Tonga SOPs with Shikha. Shadow the SC and inventory function during the Tonga entity maturity phase. Targeted readiness review at 6-month mark.
Development signal to Shikha

Julia's performance on the current KPIs — particularly debtor days, billing cycle-time, and meter reading compliance — is the leading indicator for readiness for the Operations Lead role. Green across all five KPIs for two consecutive quarters is a reasonable bar for a formal conversation about progression.
