01

Critical Success Factors

Jane's role is the NZ-side control function in the billing-to-cash chain. Her three CSFs define where she must be consistent for island team work to flow to recognised revenue.

CSF 01
Approval speed and accuracy
100% of SUBMITTED sales invoices (all five entities) reviewed and approved — or explicitly flagged with a reason — within the same working day they arrive in ApprovalMax. Delay here is invisible to island teams and creates AR ageing problems downstream.
Feeds WIG 02
CSF 02
AP triage discipline
RSP NZ purchase invoices are logged into ApprovalMax Capture the same day they arrive, with correct entity, tracking category, and approval routing. No invoice sits unprocessed past 9am the following morning.
Supports AP SOP
CSF 03
Due-date vigilance
Any purchase invoice with a due date within 3 working days is flagged to Minoo — same day. Minoo owns the payment decision; Jane owns the early warning. Missed due dates are entirely preventable.
Supports Minoo / AP SOP
