Sales Actuals — Orders object

Sales This Week / MTD / YTDLag measure
Calculation: SUM(Order.TotalAmount) where Type = 'New' AND Status = 'Activated', filtered by EffectiveDate range and Pricebook2Id for territory. Raw local currency returned; converted to NZD client-side (local ÷ FX rate).
This is actual committed revenue — not pipeline aspiration. Orders represent booked MPS hardware or services. NZ YTD shows $0 — verify NZ orders are assigned to the correct Pricebook2Id (01s7F00000IZVgzQAH).
Verification SOQL
SELECT Pricebook2Id, CurrencyIsoCode, COUNT(Id) Orders, SUM(TotalAmount) Total
FROM Order
WHERE Type = 'New'
  AND Status = 'Activated'
  AND EffectiveDate >= 2026-03-01
  AND EffectiveDate <= 2026-03-28
GROUP BY Pricebook2Id, CurrencyIsoCode

4DX Scoreboard — Pipeline Health

Pipeline Coverage RatioLead measure
Calculation: SUM(Opportunity.Amount in NZD, open opps) ÷ SUM(ForecastingQuota.QuotaAmount in NZD, Mar 2026). Open opps = IsClosed=false AND StageName != 'Closed'. 2 orphaned records with IsClosed=false but StageName='Closed' are excluded. Target: 3×. Gauge runs 0–4×.
The most important lead measure for a sales manager. In MPS, deal cycles require 3–4× pipeline to make quota. Below 2× means the WIG is already at risk regardless of rep activity this week. Currently showing ~2.7×.
-- Pipeline (open opps):
SELECT Pricebook2Id, CurrencyIsoCode, SUM(Amount) Pipeline
FROM Opportunity
WHERE IsClosed = false AND StageName != 'Closed'
GROUP BY Pricebook2Id, CurrencyIsoCode

-- Mar 2026 quota:
SELECT QuotaOwnerId, QuotaOwner.Name, QuotaAmount, CurrencyIsoCode
FROM ForecastingQuota
WHERE StartDate = 2026-03-01
Overdue Close DatesPipeline hygiene
Calculation: COUNT(Opportunity) where IsClosed=false AND StageName != 'Closed' AND CloseDate < today. Currently 649 of 751 open opps (86%).
High overdue count means reps are not maintaining close dates. It inflates pipeline and makes forecasting unreliable. Target: <10% of open opps. Recommend a monthly close-date hygiene sweep during 1:1s. Clicking this tile in the dashboard opens the full list filtered to overdue deals.
SELECT OwnerId, COUNT(Id) Overdue
FROM Opportunity
WHERE IsClosed = false
  AND StageName != 'Closed'
  AND CloseDate < TODAY
GROUP BY OwnerId
Next Step ComplianceLead measure
Calculation: Open opps with Next_Step_Date__c >= today ÷ total open opps. Currently 62/751 = 8.3%. 674 have an overdue next step; 15 have none set.
The most actionable lead measure in this Salesforce org. A rep who books the next step owns the deal. A deal with no future next step is stalled. In 4DX terms, this is the weekly behaviour score — it predicts win rate 4–6 weeks out. 8.3% is critically low. Target: above 80%. Addressed in the 1:1 agenda: "For each flagged deal, what is the next action and when will you do it?"
-- Future next step (compliant):
SELECT COUNT(Id) FROM Opportunity
WHERE IsClosed = false AND StageName != 'Closed'
  AND Next_Step_Date__c != null
  AND Next_Step_Date__c >= TODAY

-- Overdue next step:
SELECT COUNT(Id) FROM Opportunity
WHERE IsClosed = false AND StageName != 'Closed'
  AND Next_Step_Date__c != null
  AND Next_Step_Date__c < TODAY
