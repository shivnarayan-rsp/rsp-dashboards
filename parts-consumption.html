Parts and revenue are tracked via Product Requests in Salesforce. There are two types. Product Request (SLA) records represent parts consumption on SLA-covered machines — this is a cost to RSP. Service Quote (T&M) records are time-and-materials jobs where parts are charged to the customer — this is revenue. When approved, both create an Order: SLA orders are at $0 value, T&M orders are at RRP.

The objective is to reduce SLA parts spend (manage asset reliability, reduce repeat failures) and increase T&M revenue (convert more out-of-contract work into billable jobs).
